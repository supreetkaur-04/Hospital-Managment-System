About Alpha Canis Hospital

Since our founding in 1995, Alpha Canis Hospital has been at the forefront of medical innovation and compassionate care. With over 25 years of excellence, we've grown into a premier healthcare institution serving communities nationwide.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services through advanced technology, innovative treatments, and compassionate care that exceeds patient expectations and improves quality of life.

We are committed to advancing medical knowledge through research and education while maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence and patient safety.

Core Values

  • Compassion: Treating every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect
  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in clinical care and service
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and treatment methodologies
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards in all our practices
  • Community: Serving as a responsible healthcare partner in our communities
  • Collaboration: Fostering teamwork among staff for optimal patient outcomes

Our Journey Through Time

1995 - Foundation

Established as a 50-bed multi-specialty hospital with a vision to provide accessible, quality healthcare

2002 - First Expansion

Added cardiac center and neonatal ICU, becoming a regional referral center

2008 - Technological Leap

Installed first MRI and CT scan technology in the region, revolutionizing diagnostics

2013 - Academic Partnership

Launched medical residency programs in partnership with State University

2017 - New Cancer Center

Opened comprehensive cancer center with advanced radiation therapy

2020 - Pandemic Response

Designated as regional COVID-19 response center, serving over 5,000 patients

2023 - Robotic Surgery

Implemented robotic surgery program, completing 500+ successful procedures
